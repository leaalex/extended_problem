Example 1. Determine the payback period for a cogeneration power plant based on a piston engine, which is running on gaseous fuel. Initial data:

- capacity \(N_{el} = 140 \ \mathrm{kW} \);

- thermal power \(N_h = 200 \ \mathrm{kW} \);

- gas consumption \(G = 43 \ \mathrm{m^3/h} \);

- pipeline rates \(T1 = 6000 \ \mathrm{rubles/1000 \ m^3} \);

- electricity tariff \(T2 = 3 \ \mathrm{rubles/(kWh)} \);

- heat tariff \(T3 = 2000 \ \mathrm{rubles/Gcal} \);

- power plant maintenance and repair costs \(T4 = 500 \ 000 \ \mathrm{rubles/year}\);

- capital costs for the power plant and its installation \(T5 = 7 \ 000 \ 000 \ \mathrm{rubles}\).

Solution

Quantity of power, generated per year:

\(N_{\scriptsize{\sum} el} = N_{el} \cdot 6000 = 140 \cdot 6000 = 840000 \ \mathrm{kW} \), where \(6000\) is the number of operating hours of the power plant per year.

The cost of generated electricity:

\( S2 = N_{\scriptsize{\sum} el} \cdot T2 = 840000 \cdot 3 = 2 \ 520 \ 000 \ \mathrm{rubles}.\)

The amount of generated heat:

\(N_{\scriptsize{\sum} h} = N_h \cdot 6000 = 200 \cdot 6000 = 1200000 \ \mathrm{kWh} = 1031.8 \ \mathrm{Gcal}\) (given that \(1 \ \mathrm{kWh} = 860 \ \mathrm{kcal}\)).

Cost of generated heat:

\(S3 = N_{\scriptsize{\sum} h} \cdot T3 = 1031.8 \cdot 2000 = 2 \ 063 \ 600 \ \mathrm{rubles}.\)

Total cost of the energy production:

\(S_{\scriptsize{\sum}} = S2 + S3 = 2 \ 520 \ 000 + 2 \ 063 \ 600 = 4 \ 583 \ 600 \ \mathrm{rubles}.\)

Consumption of natural gas over 1 year of operation of the power plant:

\( G_{\scriptsize{\sum}} = G \cdot 6000 = 43 \cdot 6000 = 258000 \ \mathrm{m^3}.\)

Cost of natural gas over 1 year of operation of the power plant:

\( C1 = G_{\scriptsize{\sum}} \cdot T1 = 258 \cdot 6000 = 1 \ 548 \ 000 \ \mathrm{rubles}.\)

Total operating costs over 1 year:

\(C \sum = T4 + C1 = 500 \ 000 + 1 \ 548 \ 000 = 2 \ 048 \ 000 \ \mathrm{rubles}.\)

Net income from the energy production:

\( P = S_{\scriptsize{\sum}} - C_{\scriptsize{\sum}} = 4 \ 583 \ 600 - 2 \ 048 \ 000 = 2 \ 535 \ 600 \ \mathrm{rubles/year}.\)

Payback:

\(Pb = T5/P = 7 \ 000 \ 000 / 2 \ 535 \ 600 = 2.76 \ \mathrm{years}.\)
